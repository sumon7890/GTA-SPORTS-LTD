LAB

To ensure the height of quality in dyeing, GTA has a top-of-the-line laboratory having wash test, pilling test, color fastness test, shrinkage test, light test, and many other most contemporary and highly sophisticated lab testing facilities. Here, the shade of every lot of finished fabrics is verified with Data Color and Brand Color matching machines. The laboratory with all of its procedures is approved by H&M and C&A. Accreditationby ITS has been achieved successfully.

Sample Development

Research & Development (R&D) and Sample Production facilities of GTA SPORTS are among the best in the industry. These facilities immensely contribute to ensuring each piece of apparel it makes would ultimately be an identical reproduction of the approved samples.

Q.C Development
